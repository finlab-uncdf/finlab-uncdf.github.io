Pitch Prep

Having participants practice a pitch is a great way to wrap up a workshop and challenge participants to concisely convey what they've learnt.

USE CASES

  • Practicing public speaking.
  • Collecting feedback of one's progress, strategy and insights.
  • Concluding a workshop or bootcamp.

LIMITATIONS

Prepping a pitch or presentation can be both time-consuming and intimidating for participants, ample time should be given for them to complete their pitch deck and rehearse it.
