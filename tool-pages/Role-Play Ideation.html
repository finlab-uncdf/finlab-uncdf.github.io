Role-Play Ideation

About

Role-Playing allows teams to imagine their service or product in context. Often used to present prototypes or to better understand user needs the activity is a great way to engage and react to the design in a practical manner.

Use Cases

  • Testing prototypes created by the team and working through kinks they may have.
  • Understanding a user journey and investigating the key steps that constitute it.

Limitations

Some scripting or preparation should be required from the team. Improvisation can still happen but key components of an experience should be clearly mapped out to get the most out of the exercise.
