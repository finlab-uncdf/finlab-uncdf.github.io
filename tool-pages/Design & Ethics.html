Design & Ethics

Ethics in design are becoming increasingly ambiguous, it is important for service providers and business owners to spend time reflecting on the direct and indirect impact of their work, and the trade offs involved.

USE CASES

  • Challenge the ethics behind a successful product or service.
  • Sensitise teams to the possible impact of their work and their accountability towards their users.
  • Define where to draw the line between indication and manipulation.

LIMITATIONS

Speaking about ethics can be a challenging topic, the facilitator should make sure to leave ample time for the sharing of lived experiences and for the group to reflect on these experiences.
