Design & Ethics

About

Ethics in design are becoming increasingly ambiguous, it is important to help service providers and business owners spend time reflecting on the direct and indirect impacts of their work.

Use Cases

  • Challenging the ethic behind a product or service.
  • Sensitising teams to the possible impact of their work and their accountability towards their users.
  • Defining where to draw the line between indication and manipulation.

Limitations

Speaking about ethics can be a challenging topic, the facilitator should make sure to leave ample time for the sharing of lived experiences and for the group to reflect on t hese experiences.
