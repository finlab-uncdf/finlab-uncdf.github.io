Design & Ethics

This is a short primer for organisations to consider the ethics of their solutions. As ethics in business and design become increasingly difficult to manoeuvre, it is important that service providers spend time reflecting on their impact.

USE CASES

  • Challenge the ethics behind a successful product or service.
  • Sensitise teams to the possible impact of their work and their accountability towards their users.
  • Define where to draw the line between assistance and manipulation.
