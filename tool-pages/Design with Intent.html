BEHAVIOURS & DESIGN | UNDERSTANDING BEHAVIOUR CHANGE

Design with Intent

The ‘Design with Intent’ toolkit consisting of case studies that use design to induce certain behaviours.

USE CASES

  • Diversifying one's thinking.
  • Finding relevant instances of behaviour change drivers to apply to one's work.
  • Learning to recognise such approaches in one's day to day.

LIMITATIONS

This tool is fairly exhaustive and uses very specific case studies which may not always feel relevant to everyone. The session should therefore be heavily facilitated and gamified to encourage participants to apply it to their needs.
