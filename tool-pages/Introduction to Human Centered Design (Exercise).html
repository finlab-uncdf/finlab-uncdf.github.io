Introduction to Human Centered Design (Exercise)

About

This is a short introduction to the HCD method through a workshop exercise format. It takes participants through a quick journey of the 5 stages of HCD in a light manner - focusing on the essence of the process.

Use Cases

  • Introducing people to the concept of HCD through a light exercise.

Limitations

The material for this session is aimed to give a brief overview of the HCD method in an experiential way. It is a light exercise and does not cover all tools. For further theory and process there are more details in the Introduction to HCD presentation slides.
