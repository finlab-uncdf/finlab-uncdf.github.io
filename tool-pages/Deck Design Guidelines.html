Deck Design Guidelines

About

The Deck Design Guidelines can help participants to design visually appealing decks for their pitch presentations. The guidelines contain simple tips which are simple to implement.

Use Cases

  • Creating visually appealing decks.
  • Fixing and checking decks for basics of readability and visual balance.

Limitations

Although these guidelines can help make decks look visually good, they are not all-encompassing and could be ignored in favour of thematic decks designed by professional designers. Use this deck to ensure the basic of readability, visual balance and neatness are met.
