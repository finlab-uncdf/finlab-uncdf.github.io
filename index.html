UNCDF Toolkit

The Financial Innovation Lab Toolkit

The Financial Innovation Toolkit was created to champion strategic and user-centered innovation in the financial inclusion space. This collection of tools aims to provide support to the various strategic and design needs of innovators, service providers and advocates active in the financial inclusion space. This project is the result of a partnership between UNCDF and Quicksand Design Studio.
