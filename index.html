UNCDF Toolkit

Recommended Journeys

The HCD section of the toolkit is in itself fairly comprehensive in terms of tools and can take multiple forms depending on the context and time available. The facilitation team will have to choose which tools/exercises to run based on their own preference and judgement. Given below are three journeys for different contexts. You can use these and edit them as you see fit.

Startup Innovation Lab

A short primer on HCD ideal for a startup hoping to better understand its users and how to meet their needs.

Spanning over 3 Days, this journey is better suited for Startups

Day 1

  1. Introduction to Human Centered Design

  2. Problem Tree + 5 Whys

  3. Startup Canvas

  4. Journey Map

Day 2

  1. Secondary Research

  2. User Research

  3. Affinity Mapping

  4. Design Principles

Day 3

  1. What If

  2. SCAMPER

  3. Storyboarding

  4. Role-Playing

  5. Theory of Change

  6. SMART Goals
